Nourishment Recipe Book

I designed and produce a printed recipe book containing three recipes that say something about me and in particular three stages of my life; childhood, teenage years and adulthood. Each recipe is accompanied by a short essay that explains why this recipe or food was important in that stage of my life. The three recipes are almost like 3 lessons that I learnt in each stage that shaped the way I am today.
